# ARUNA NETWORK

## MASTER PRD

### Volume 10 — Ecosystem, Governance, Treasury & Community Growth

---

# ECOSYSTEM PHILOSOPHY

ARUNA is designed as a complete community-driven ecosystem. The long-term goal is to grow developers, miners, validators, builders, businesses, and communities that collectively maintain and expand the network.

---

# GOVERNANCE MODEL

ARUNA adopts **Hybrid Governance** which combines Stake Voting, Proposal Systems, and Time-Delayed Execution.

### Evolution Stages:
* **Stage 1:** Founder Guided
* **Stage 2:** Community Assisted
* **Stage 3:** Community Governed

### Governance Boundaries:
* **Governance Controls:** Treasury disbursements, protocol upgrades, network parameters, and ecosystem grants.
* **Governance CANNOT Change:** Maximum token supply (1B ARU), founder vesting rules, or historical transactions.

---

# PROPOSAL LIFECYCLE & TIMELOCKS

Proposals must proceed through the following path:
```
Proposal Created → Discussion → Review → Voting → Timelock (7 Days) → Execution
```
* **Timelock:** All accepted proposals must enter a **7-day timelock** before execution to prevent governance flash loans or hostile capture.

### Proposal Thresholds:
* **Governance Proposal:** Minimum threshold of **10,000 ARU**.
* **Treasury Proposal:** Minimum threshold of **50,000 ARU**.
* **Protocol Proposal:** Minimum threshold of **100,000 ARU** (Requires a complete Architecture Decision Record - ADR).

---

# TREASURY SCHEME

* **Treasury Philosophy:** Treasury exists solely to fund infrastructure (seed nodes, RPC, explorer), audits, bug bounties, developer grants, and education. It is not founder income.
* **Funding Sources:** 5% of block rewards, supplemented by future network activity (DEX trading fees, Launchpad fees, Domain Registration fees, and Bridge fees).
* **Transparency:** Treasury must publish monthly, quarterly, and annual public reports of all transaction movements.

---

# COMMUNITY & DEVELOPER ENGAGEMENT

* **Miner Program:** Promote nationwide mining via setup guides, contests, and community pools targeting Android, Raspberry Pi, Mini PCs, and ARM servers.
* **Validator Support:** Detailed validator documentation, dashboards, and automated performance logs.
* **Developer Ecosystem:** SDK/API tooling in Rust, TypeScript, JavaScript, and Dart.
* **Grants Program:** Funded by the treasury to bootstrap wallets, tools, explorers, analytics, and open-source libraries.

---

# ROADMAP SUCCESS METRICS

### Milestones:
* **Year 1 (Sumatera):** 100 Nodes, 20 Validators, 1,000 Wallets (Explorer, Mobile Miner Prototype, Wallet MVP).
* **Year 3 (Sulawesi):** 1,000 Nodes, 100 Validators, 25,000 Wallets (EVM Layer, DEX MVP).
* **Year 5 (Jawa Mainnet):** 5,000 Nodes, 500 Validators, 250,000 Wallets (Global Ecosystem Launch).

### Decentralization Targets:
* **Validator Stake Cap:** No single validator should control **>10%** of the total staked weight.
* **Mining Hashrate Cap:** No single mining pool should control **>25%** of the total network hashrate.
* **Auditing:** Treasury spending must remain 100% public and auditable.

---

# SUCCESS CRITERIA

Ecosystem and governance succeed when:
1. The network survives and progresses independently of the founder.
2. Staking and validating remains decentralized.
3. Developers continue building.
4. Treasury operations are sustainable and transparent.
5. Community governance functions effectively.
