### Volume 2 — Tokenomics & Economic System

---

# ECONOMIC PHILOSOPHY

ARUNA adopts a sustainability-first economic model.
The network is designed to survive without:
* Venture Capital dependency
* Centralized funding
* Foundation-controlled inflation
* Continuous external capital injection

The economic system prioritizes:
1. Fair Distribution
2. Community Ownership
3. Long-Term Sustainability
4. Low Entry Barrier
5. Incentive Alignment

# HALVING MODEL

Objective:
Mimic Bitcoin-style scarcity while maintaining sustainable network incentives.

Halving Interval:
4 Years

Blocks Per Era:
4,204,800

* **Era 1 (Years 0–4):** 25 ARU
* **Era 2 (Years 4–8):** 12.5 ARU
* **Era 3 (Years 8–12):** 6.25 ARU
* **Era 4 (Years 12–16):** 3.125 ARU
* **Era 5 (Years 16–20):** 1.5625 ARU

*Emission continues until approaching maximum supply.*

# MONETARY POLICY

ARUNA must never:
* Print additional supply
* Modify supply cap
* Introduce hidden inflation
* Create emergency mint functions

Supply rules are immutable protocol rules.

# OFFLINE VALIDATOR POLICY

ARUNA follows a community-friendly approach.
* **No slashing.**
* Offline validators lose rewards while offline.
* Offline validators can return later.
* No stake destruction.
* No punitive slashing mechanism.
